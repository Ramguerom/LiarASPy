Here are our teachers:

Ms. Cornelia M. Guerrero Romero

She was born in Jerez de la Frontera, begins her musical studies in a small academy. Years later, he took the entrance test to the Conservatory of Music, Joaquin Villatoro, obtaining one of the best grades in his class. After finishing his professional degree, he studied at the "Manuel Castillo" Higher Conservatory of Music in Seville, winning renowned competitions during those years.
She has won national competitions from a very young age, making her a name known throughout the country. He teaches numerous courses and master classes in different cities inside and outside our borders.


Mr. Ramón J. Guerrero Romero

Born in Jerez de la Frontera, he began his piano studies at the Joaquin Villatoro Conservatory. After completing the professional degree, he completed and completed his studies at the Conservatorio Superior de Música in Madrid.
He has taken advanced courses and received master classes from Vladimir Tropp, Konstantin Shamray, Elena Galynina, Johnn Salmon, Giuseppe Maiorca, Ángel Sanzo and Nino Kereselidze among others.
Since he was little he has also been passionate about singing, in such a way that he is enrolled in the Villamarta Theater choir helping other people who are also passionate about singing to do it in a more appropriate way. In the same way, he has participated in different opera with singers such as Ismael Yordi, David Lagos, David de Maria among others.


Sr. Martín A. Guerrero Romero

Born in Jerez de la Frontera, he began his piano studies at the Joaquin Villatoro Conservatory. After finishing his professional degree, he studied at the Conservatorio Superior de Música de Granada.
He forms and directs the choir "Los Monchitos", representing different operas such as "La Bohème", "Tosca", "La Flauta Mágica", "Turandot", "Diluvio de Noé", among others, in different theaters such as the Villamarta Theater and the Teatro de la Maestranza (Seville).
Aside from the great pianist he is, he is also a magnificent composer and arranger. He likes it and is so good at it that he has even composed an opera which was premiered last August in 2018 at the Manuel de Falla Theater.
